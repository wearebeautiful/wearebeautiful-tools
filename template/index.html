Welcome!

We are Beautiful ... is a project designed to lift the mystery that surrounds human genitalia. In many cultures and through a lot of human history it has been taboo to look at other human genitalia.

We aim to change this by presenting educational, respectul and representative models of typical humans. You may browse the collection of 3D models and you can even download and print these models on your own 3D printer.

Our goal is to provide educational models that sex educators everywhere can download, print and use in their classes. Also, if you have questions about your own genitalia, have a look at other humans -- we come in many different shapes and sizes.

Click on any model to view the 3D version of it or to download the model. We encourage you to find out more about this project and our team. Also, please consider making a small donation to help cover our operating costs.

PS: This site is in ALPHA condition -- there are many tweaks that need to be made to improve the site and we are far from ready for release. We're making this site available to trusted friends in order to help us overcome basic hurdles in getting the company/site off the ground. Please respect the models who have contributed to this site by not downloading any models or making any snapshots of the site.
